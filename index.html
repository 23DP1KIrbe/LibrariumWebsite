About App

Librarium is your all-in-one book app to discover, organize, and track your reading journey. Browse thousands of books, sort by genre or rating, and save them to custom lists like Want to Read or Favorites. Rate books, write reviews, and find purchase options—all in one place. Perfect for book lovers who want a smarter way to explore literature.
